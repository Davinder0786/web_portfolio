ACADEMIC HISTORY

Undergraduate Degree

I pursued my undergraduate studies in Computer Science at the University of Staffordshire University, located in UK. The program spanned over three years, during which I gained a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of computer science and software engineering.

The curriculum included a range of modules, covering subjects such as algorithms, data structures, software development methodologies, and computer networks. Additionally, I had the opportunity to work on several practical projects that enhanced my problem-solving skills and allowed me to apply theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios.

Throughout my undergraduate studies, I actively participated in extracurricular activities, including coding competitions and hackathons, which not only enriched my technical skills but also provided valuable networking opportunities within the tech community.

Graduate Degree

For my graduate studies, I pursued a Master's degree in Information Technology at Staffordshire University, based in the United Kingdom. The program was a transformative experience, spanning one year and providing in-depth knowledge in various facets of information technology.

The curriculum at Staffordshire University emphasized advanced topics in software engineering, database management, cybersecurity, and information systems. I engaged in research-oriented projects that allowed me to delve into emerging technologies, such as machine learning and data analytics, contributing to my analytical and problem-solving capabilities.

Collaborating with experienced faculty and fellow students, I gained practical insights into industry trends and best practices. The program also included opportunities for internships and industry partnerships, enabling me to apply theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios and fostering a strong connection between academia and industry.
